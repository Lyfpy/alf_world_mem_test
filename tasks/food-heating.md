## When to Use
for tasks that involve heating food items (typically bread or similar items) using microwave before placing them at a destination

## Knowledge
microwave is primary heating appliance, food items found on countertops, need to open microwave before heating, heated items can be placed at various destinations

## SOP
1) Find target food item, 2) Take food item, 3) Go to microwave, 4) Open microwave, 5) Heat food item with microwave, 6) Go to destination, 7) Place heated food item
